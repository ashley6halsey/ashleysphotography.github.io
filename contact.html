Content and Pricing

Photoshoots for any occassion! Graduation, portraits, special events, and creative shoots. Any location of your choosing within 20 miles of Los Angeles. Two to three weeks following the session, you will be emailed a google drive folder with all of the edited photos from your shoot. Typical hour long photo sessions produce between 40 and 70 images, but if we happen to take more, you'll recieve them all.

Most photoshoots are scheduled during the day and early evening to take advantage of the sunset golden hour. In the spring months during graduation season, popular campus locations get pretty busy with everyone trying to get their photos taken at the same time. This can often limit the amount of shots we take. If you prefer a more intimate setting at a time with less people we can schedule an early morning sunrise golden hour photoshoot for an additional cost (+$100)

Sessions are booked through a google form linked here (and the bottom of this page)! But feel free to contact me with any questions or booking information at: ashleycarpenter@ucla.edu
