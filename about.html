Meet Your Photographer

Photography has always been a passion of mine. I was always the person snapping away, taking candid photos of my friends with a phone camera. In high school, I discovered my love for the physics of taking photos. I was able to experiment in pinhole photography with a shoebox camera I made in my physics class. Since then I have improved my skills and equipment. For the last 4 years I've been shooting primarily with a Canon Rebel T7i, photographing friends and even doing self portraits! Aside from taking photos, I'm also an Astrophysics student in my 4th year at UCLA.

My specialty is my ability to capture the moment. The candid smiles and laughter show some of the happiest moments. If you tend to be camera shy do not worry! I always come prepared with some guidance. Our main priority during the photoshoot is to have a fun time. By the end you'll have a wonderful selection of unique images.
