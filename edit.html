Editing Styles

Below are some of my personal editing styles. Over the last 3 and half years I have curated a variety of Lightroom presets and techniques to enhance the overall mood of a photo. "Standard" tends to be the default editing style for most photoshoots, but if you're looking for something a little less conventional, I am more more than happy to be flexible!

Standard

Natural hues and tones. Captures a more realistic interpretation of what it was like to be there "in the moment."

Soft

Low contrast softens features of the subject, creating an light and airy aura. Cooler shadows with warm highlights to create a dreamy snapshot of time.

Dramatic

High contrast with deep shadows creates a more mysterious mood, demanding attention towards the subject.
